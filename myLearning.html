    2. Udemy is a great website that feature courses on majority of computer science concepts.   3. W3Schools

    4. W3Schools is one of the best websites that I have come accross to learn programming, They have information on a multitude of languages and provide a very clear description of the concept and make the concepts much more easier to understand with appropriate examples, tutorials and exercises. It has basically been the one stop solution for me whenever I forget any concept or want to learn any new ones. Click on the logo below to open their website.


  4. Typing Club

    5. Being able to type fast can be very useful skill when programming, but the reason I wanted to learn touch typing was to type without having to look at my keyboard constantly. Typing club is a great free online resource which helped me greatly increase my touch typing speed in only a few months, I can now write upto almost 70 WPM (Words Per Minute).
